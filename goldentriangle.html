Golden Triangle Tour Package

5 Night / 6 Days Delhi - Agra - Jaipur

Tour Overview

Experience the essence of India's cultural diversity and historical grandeur with our Golden Triangle Tour Package. This 6-day journey takes you through the three iconic cities of Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur, offering a kaleidoscope of experiences from the Mughal era to the Rajput Kings.

Travel Itinerary

Day 1
Arrival Delhi - Half Day Sightseeing
  • Meet and greet at airport/hotel
  • Visit Red Fort & Raj Path
  • India Gate war memorial
  • Shopping at Connaught Place
Day 2
Delhi Local Full Day Sightseeing
  • Qutub Minar Complex
  • Lotus Temple & Humayun’s Tomb
  • Akshardham Temple visit
Day 3
Delhi – Agra (Taj Mahal City)
  • Drive to Agra
  • Visit the iconic Taj Mahal
  • Explore Agra Fort & Mehtab Bagh
Day 4
Agra – Fatehpur Sikri – Jaipur
  • En route visit Fatehpur Sikri (Ghost City)
  • Check-in at Jaipur (The Pink City)
Day 5
Jaipur City Sightseeing
  • Amer Fort & City Palace
  • Jantar Mantar & Hawa Mahal
  • Jal Mahal in Man Sagar Lake
  • Evening at Chokhi Dhani (Optional)
Day 6
Departure to Delhi

After breakfast, drive back to Delhi and transfer to Airport/Railway station for onward journey.

What's Included

Accommodation on Twin Sharing.
MAP Plan (Breakfast & Dinner).
AC Vehicle for all transfers.
Licensed Guide in Agra & Jaipur.
