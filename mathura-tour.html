Golden Triangle with Mathura

6 Night / 7 Days Delhi-Mathura-Agra-Jaipur

Tour Overview

This package combines the historical richness of the Golden Triangle with the spiritual aura of Mathura, the birthplace of Lord Krishna. Experience majestic forts, serene temples, and the eternal beauty of the Taj Mahal.

Travel Itinerary

Day 1
Arrival Delhi & Half Day Tour
  • Check-in at Delhi hotel
  • Visit Red Fort & India Gate
  • Shopping at Connaught Place
Day 2
Delhi – Mathura
  • Drive to the holy city of Mathura
  • Visit Prem Mandir & Krishna Janam Asthan
  • Explore Dwarkadish & ISKCON Temple
Day 3
Mathura – Agra
  • Drive to Taj City Agra
  • Visit Taj Mahal & Agra Fort
  • Sunset view from Mehtab Bagh
Day 4
Agra – Fatehpur Sikri – Jaipur
  • Explore the ghost city of Fatehpur Sikri
  • Bulk transfer to the Pink City Jaipur
Day 5
Jaipur City Sightseeing
  • Amer Fort & City Palace
  • Hawa Mahal & Jal Mahal
  • Evening culture at Chokhi Dhani
Day 6
Jaipur – Delhi
  • Drive back to Delhi
  • Visit Qutub Minar & Akshardham Temple
Day 7
Delhi Drop

Final transfer to Delhi Airport / Railway station.

What's Included

  • AC Vehicle for entire trip
  • 6 Nights stay with Breakfast
