Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about booking with Sight Holidays

How can I book a holiday package with Sight Holidays?

You can book a package by browsing our website, selecting your preferred destination, and clicking on 'Request Price' or contacting us directly via WhatsApp.

Do you offer customized travel packages?

Yes, we specialize in customized tour packages tailored to your preferences, budget, and travel dates. Just let us know your requirements!

What are the best honeymoon destinations in India?

Popular honeymoon destinations include Kashmir, Kerala (Munnar/Alleppey), Andaman Islands, Goa, and Manali. We offer special romantic inclusions for honeymooners.

Is it safe to travel to Kashmir?

Yes, Kashmir is a major tourist hub and is safe for tourists. We ensure your stay is in safe and verified hotels and houseboats.

Can I facilitate flights through you?

Our packages primarily include land arrangements (Hotel, Cab, Sightseeing). However, we can assist you with flight bookings upon request.

What documents do I need for international travel?

For international tours, you generally need a passport with at least 6 months validity, a valid visa (depending on the destination), and travel insurance. We assist our clients with visa guidance and documentation.

Does Sight Holidays provide group tour packages?

Yes, we specialize in organizing group tours, corporate retreats, and family reunions. Our group packages often come with special discounts and customized itineraries.

Are there any hidden costs in the tour packages?

We believe in transparent pricing. All inclusions and exclusions are clearly mentioned in the package itinerary. There are no hidden charges; however, personal expenses like shopping or extra meals are not covered.

What is the best time to visit Himachal Pradesh and Kashmir?

The best time to visit Himachal and Kashmir is from March to June for pleasant weather, and from December to February if you want to enjoy snow and winter sports.

Do you offer solo traveler friendly packages?

Absolutely! We design safe and engaging solo traveler packages with verified accommodations and shared group activities to ensure a comfortable experience for single travelers.
